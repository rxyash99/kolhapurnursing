Nursing & Clinical

Professional Nursing Care at Home

Our qualified nurses, doctors and clinical professionals bring hospital-standard care directly to your loved one's bedside.

👩‍⚕️

Qualified Nurses, Ayas & GDA / Attendants

Certified, experienced nursing professionals assigned to provide attentive, round-the-clock bedside care tailored to your patient's specific medical condition. All staff are background-verified with transparent profiles.

🤝

Patient Attendants

Trained personal attendants offering compassionate support for daily activities including feeding, mobility, hygiene, and emotional companionship — ensuring your loved one never feels alone.

🧑‍🦱

Ayas / GDA (General Duty Attendant)

Skilled GDAs handle daily patient care tasks under nursing supervision — grooming, feeding assistance, basic vitals monitoring and patient comfort management throughout the day.

🏠

ICU Development at Home

We set up a full intensive care unit environment in your home — ventilators, monitors, suction machines and round-the-clock trained nursing supervision for critically ill patients.

🛁

Body Care

Complete hygienic body care including sponge baths, oral hygiene, pressure sore prevention, skin care and regular patient repositioning to prevent bedsores during prolonged recovery.

🧘

Yoga & Healing

Therapeutic yoga sessions and holistic healing practices tailored to recovery needs. Gentle stretching, breathing exercises and mind-body techniques to support faster recuperation.

👨‍⚕️

Doctor's Visit at Home

Experienced registered doctors visit your home for medical consultations, follow-up check-ups, prescription management and clinical assessments — no need to strain for hospital trips.

🦴

Physiotherapy — All Specialities

Certified physiotherapists for orthopaedic, neurological, cardiac, paediatric and geriatric rehabilitation — personalised exercise programs and manual therapy for complete recovery.

💉

Medications & Injections (IV, IM, SC)

Safe, precise administration of intravenous, intramuscular and subcutaneous medications and drips. Specialised experience with IVF injections requiring delicate, gentle technique.

🩹

Dressing & Wound Care

Professional sterile wound dressing, post-surgical wound management and chronic wound care. Our nurses follow clinical protocols to prevent infection and promote optimal healing.

🦵

Plasters

Expert application, management and removal of plaster casts for fractures and orthopaedic conditions, carried out with professional care and clinical oversight.

🔧

Enema

Medically administered enema procedures for constipation management, bowel preparation and clinical indications — performed with full dignity and professional clinical technique.

🫁

Tracheostomy Tube Care

Specialised tracheostomy care including tube suctioning, inner cannula cleaning, stoma dressing and airway management by nurses trained in critical care procedures.

🍽️

PEG Feeding

Percutaneous endoscopic gastrostomy tube feeding with accurate nutritional delivery, site care, tube patency maintenance and clinical monitoring for patients unable to eat orally.

🧪

Ryles Tube Insertion

Nasogastric tube insertion, placement verification and ongoing care management for patients requiring tube feeding or gastric decompression under clinical supervision.

🫀

Foley's Catheterisation

Sterile urinary catheter insertion and comprehensive ongoing catheter care management by skilled nurses — preventing infection and maintaining patient comfort and dignity.

💧

Parental Nutrition

Intravenous parenteral nutritional support for patients unable to absorb nutrients orally — administered with clinical precision by our trained nursing team at home.

👴

Senior Citizen Care

Comprehensive elderly care services: gentle sponge baths, personal grooming, diaper change assistance, walking support and safety monitoring — preserving independence and dignity.

🏥

Hospitalisation Assistance

A dedicated attendant during hospital stays to coordinate care, communicate with medical teams, provide family updates and ensure round-the-clock patient comfort and advocacy.

❤️

ECG (Electrocardiogram)

12-lead electrocardiogram recordings performed at home using professional-grade equipment, with results shared promptly with your treating cardiologist for evaluation.

💊

Medicine Home Delivery

Timely delivery of prescribed medications directly to your doorstep — ensuring uninterrupted treatment schedules without the difficulty of pharmacy visits during recovery.

🩸

Blood Transfusion

Supervised blood transfusion services at home with continuous vital signs monitoring, reaction surveillance and emergency preparedness by our clinical nursing team.

🔬

Blood Sample Collection

Home-visit phlebotomy for all laboratory tests — quick, comfortable and hygienic sample collection with proper cold-chain handling for accurate test results.
