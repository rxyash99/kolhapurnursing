Who We Are

Care Born from Conviction

Kolhapur Nursing Bureau was founded with a singular vision: to ensure that no family in Kolhapur ever has to choose between quality medical care and the comfort of home. We saw patients suffering unnecessary distress from repeated hospital visits, families struggling with the logistics of inpatient care — and we believed there was a better way.

"Every service you get in a hospital, we provide at your home — from professional nursing staff, caretakers and attendants, to doctors — at the best and most reasonable rates."

Today, we are widely known throughout Kolhapur and nearby areas for our transparent staff profiles, flexible duty arrangements and modified service packages. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all — every patient is unique, and every care plan should reflect that.

Rated 4.7★ on Google with over 200+ reviews, our reputation is built not on marketing — but on the genuine satisfaction of families who've trusted us in their most vulnerable moments.
